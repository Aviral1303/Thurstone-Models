Caveat — rank CIs only

A minority of bootstrap replicates diverge (centered abilities explode when a replicate's resampled prices are degenerate), blowing the skill quantiles out to ±10⁹ while the rank quantiles stay sane. Skill CIs from this run are unreportable; every interval above is a rank CI. Cotton-side bots sit in a single resampling cluster, so their bootstrap coverage is ≈ 0.64 and their CIs are lower bounds.

What the system itself revealed

Four pathologies of MultiRayGlobalCalibrator on small data — as important as the board, all documented in logs/BOT_ARENA_MULTIRAY_FINDINGS.md

  1. Beta rides a translation ridge.

    Race prices depend only on relative abilities within a condition, so the per-condition offset is unidentified — one seed drifted to |β| ≈ 10¹³ at zero fit cost. Any consumer of raw abilities must center within condition first.

  2. The alternating optimizer is non-monotone and a seed lottery.

    Price MSE went 2.0e-4 → 1.8e-2 between outer iterations for one seed; three starts landed at MSE {7.9e-5, 3.6e-3, 2.2e-1}. The harness wraps the fit with best-iterate snapshots + best-of-seeds selection — the model itself is untouched.

  3. The embedding geometry is unidentified at this data size.

    ~12 free price observations vs ~16+ parameters at dim = 2 puts (Z, V) on a solution manifold; the naive mean-ray projection was seed-dependent (τ from −0.33 to 0.33). Ranking uses the identified summary instead: race-weighted, within-condition-centered ability.

  4. Winner-take-all prices discard information the pairwise view keeps.

    Only the race winner enters the likelihood — second-vs-third never does, and 2-race fields fall below the dense-run threshold entirely. Script 33's pairwise decomposition uses all of it; this is the likely source of the metals-tail permutation.

Bottom line

With 4–11 bots and 8–26 conditions there is not enough data to identify a genuine second skill dimension — the extra freedom shows up as optimizer pathology, not insight. The one genuinely new view is the per-condition ability decomposition: commodity_spot_1's edge is large in 2-bot palladium/silver fields (win prob ≈ 0.65–0.83) but drops below diffusion_mc_1 and pair_trading_1 in the 4-bot silver field (≈ 0.21).

Not yet in the paper — the multiray results live in the findings log and the web boards only.
